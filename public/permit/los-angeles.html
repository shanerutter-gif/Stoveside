Los Angeles-specific notes

What's different about Los Angeles.

  • LA County's MEHKO is a pilot, currently authorized through June 2026 with strong signals it will be made permanent.
  • Plan-check fee is one-time at approval; renewal years are just the permit fee.
  • LA has the largest MEHKO applicant pool in the state — inspection scheduling can run slower than smaller counties.

Frequently asked

Questions about Los Angeles MEHKO.

Is LA County MEHKO actually permanent yet?

Not yet — it is still a pilot authorized through June 2026. The Board of Supervisors is expected to vote on permanent status before the pilot expires, with strong early signals pointing to continuation.

What is the plan-check fee for?

LA requires a one-time plan review of your kitchen layout and SOP before issuing an operational permit. After year one, you just pay the annual permit fee on renewal.

Can I operate in the City of LA or just unincorporated areas?

Both. LA County DPH-EH covers the unincorporated county and all cities that contract public health services with the county — which is most of LA County.
