Frequently asked

Questions about Riverside MEHKO.

Why is Riverside the oldest MEHKO program?

After AB 626 became law in 2018, Riverside's Board of Supervisors was the first to pass an implementing ordinance. They've been issuing MEHKO permits since 2019 and have the most operational experience of any California county.

Does Riverside cap the number of MEHKO permits?

No — there is no quota. Every applicant who meets the standards and passes inspection receives a permit. AB 626 prohibits counties from capping program size.

Can I operate across county lines?

No. Your permit is valid only in the county where it was issued, and only at the residence listed on the permit.
